Gel-fixing solution
===================

* Final concentration = Methanol, 50% (v/v) ethanol; Glacial acetic acid, 10% (v/v)

**Materials**

* Ethanol
* Glacial acetic acid 
* Distilled H2O  

**Procedure**

#. Add 50 mL of ethanol and 10 mL of acetic acid into 40 mL of distilled H2O. 
#. Mix well.
#. Label and store at bench.
