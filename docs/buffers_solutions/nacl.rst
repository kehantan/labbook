Sodium chloride (NaCl)
======================
 
* Concentration = 5 M
* NaCl MW: 58.44 g/mol

**Materials**

* Sodium chloride (NaCl) 
* Empty bottle
* Disitlled H2O
* Magnetic stirrer 

**Procedure**

#. Measure 73.05 g of NaCl into about 150-200 mL of distilled H2O. 
#. Stir with a magnetic stirrer until NaCl completely dissolved. 
#. Top up the NaCl solution with distilled H2O up to 250 mL. 
#. Label the bottle. 

    * Label example: :code:`NaCl, 5M, name initials, date prepared`

#. Store at bench.
