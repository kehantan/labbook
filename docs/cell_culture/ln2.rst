Liquid nitrogen (LN2) storage 
=====================

* LN2 are meant for long term storage of cells. For short term storage (< 6 mth), can keep it in -80 °C
* There is a LN2 tank in the lab. Remember to check the LN2 level once a week, we prefer to keep the volume of LN2 above 10 cm high
* When storing cells, remember to fill in the form which record which tower and which box are your cells being stored at in the form
* Remember to check and secure the retention bar/locking rod before placing the tower back into the LN2 to prevent the cryoboxes from falling into the LN2. Cryoboxes that dropped into the LN2 is extremely tedious to retrieve
* Remember to check the LN2 tank is properly closed after you take out/store your cells to prevent the LN2 from drying up
