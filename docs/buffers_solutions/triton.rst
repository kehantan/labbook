Triton X-100
============

* Triton X-100 concentration = 10% (v/v)

**Materials**

* Triton X-100 (100%)
* Distilled H2O
* Centrifuge tube
* Micropipette 
* Micropipette tips 

**Procedure**

#. In a centrifuge tube, add 1 mL of Triton X-100 (100%) solution into 5 mL distilled H2O.
#. Pipette repeatedly to mix Triton X-100 with distilled H2O. Use a vortex if necessary. 

    * Triton X-100 is very viscous and can be a little bit difficult to mix with water at the beginning. 
    * Vortex will cause excessive bubbling but the bubbles will disappear after left to sit on bench for some time. 

#. Top up with distilled H2O until final volume is 10 mL.
