Gel-destaining solution
=======================

* Final concentration = Methanol, 50% (v/v); Glacial acetic acid, 10% (v/v)

**Materials**

* Methanol
* Glacial acetic acid
* Distilled H2O 

**Procedure**

#. Add 50 mL of methanol and 10 mL of glacial acetic acid to 40 mL of distilled H2O. 
#. Mix well. 
#. Label and store at bench.
