Glycerol solution
=================

* Concentration = 80% (v/v)
* May adjust percentage accordingly. 

**Materials**

* 100% glycerol 
* Distilled H2O
* Bottle

**Procedure** 

#. Add 80 mL of 100% glycerol into a empty bottle. 
#. Add 20 mL of distilled water into 100% glycerol. 
#. Autoclave.
#. Store at bench.
