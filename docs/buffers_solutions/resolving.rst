Resolving buffer
================

* Tris base concentration = 1.5 M
* Resolving buffer final pH = 8.8 
* Tris MW = 121.14 g/mol

**Materials** 

* Tris base
* Distilled H2O 
* Hydrochloric acid (HCl)
* pH meter 

**Procedure** 

#. Add 18.17 g of Tris base into 60-80 mL of distilled water. 

    * 1 M = 121.14 g/L; 1.5 M = 181.71 g/L = 18.171 g/100 mL

#. Stir untill Tris base powder completely dissolved. 
#. Adjust pH to 8.8 with HCl.
#. Top up with distilled water untill 100 mL. 
#. Label and store at 4 C. 

**Note:** HCl fuming at 37% (undiluted), better dilute to less than 20% to reduce hazard risk.
