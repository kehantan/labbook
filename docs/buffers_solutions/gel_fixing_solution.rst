Gel-fixing solution
===================

**Objective:** To prepare fixing solution for SDS-PAGE.

**Requires**

* Ethanol, 95% 
* MilliQ water or distilled water  
* Acetic acid 

**Procedure**

#. Add 50 mL of 95% ethanol and 10 mL of acetic acid into 40 mL of distilled water. 
#. Mix well.
