Buffer B
=========
This is a preparation upon Dr. Mori's request: 
100 mM Tris-HCl 
pH 8.0
300 mM NaCl
6M Urea

Formula
g = M x L x MW

g = weight in grams
M = Molar 
L = Water in Litres
MW = Molecular weight

To make 250 ml of Buffer B:
---------------------------
Weight 90.06 g of Urea in an empty bottle.
