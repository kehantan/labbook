Gel-washing solution
====================

**Objective:** To prepare washing solution for SDS-PAGE.

**Requires**

* Methanol 
* MilliQ water or distilled water  
* Acetic acid 

**Procedure**

#. Add 50 mL of methanol and 10 mL of acetic acid into 40 mL of distilled water. 
#. Mix well.
