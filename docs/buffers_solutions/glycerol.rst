Glycerol solution
=================

**Objective:** To prepare glycerol solution for bacteria stock. 

**Materials**

* 100% glycerol 
* Distilled water
* Empty glass bottle 

**Procedure** 

#. Add 80 mL of 100% glycerol into a 250 mL empty glass bottle. 
#. Add 20 mL of distilled water. 
#. Autoclave.
#. Store at bench. 

* May adjust percentage accordingly.
